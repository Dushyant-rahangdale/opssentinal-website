# Mobile Setup (PWA + Push)

Get OpsKnight running on your phone in minutes. Install the PWA, enable push notifications, and
start responding on the go.

![OpsKnight mobile dashboard](/mobile-dashboard.png)

---

## 1) Install the PWA

### iOS (Safari)
1. Open OpsKnight in Safari
2. Tap the **Share** button (square with arrow)
3. Tap **Add to Home Screen**
4. Tap **Add**

### Android (Chrome)
1. Open OpsKnight in Chrome
2. Tap the **three-dot menu**
3. Tap **Add to Home Screen** or **Install App**
4. Tap **Install**

### Desktop (Chrome/Edge)
1. Open OpsKnight in your browser
2. Click the **install** icon in the address bar
3. Click **Install**

---

## 2) Enable Push Notifications

When prompted, tap **Allow** for notifications. Then verify your device is registered:

1. Go to **Profile → Devices**
2. Confirm your device appears in the list
3. Ask your admin to enable **Push** in **Settings → Notification Providers**

![OpsKnight push notification on iOS](/push-notification.png)

---

## 3) Test Delivery

- Trigger a test incident or use a test alert integration
- Confirm you receive a push notification
- Open the incident from the notification and acknowledge it

---

## Troubleshooting

- **No prompt?** Ensure you installed the PWA and notifications are allowed in OS settings.
- **No device listed?** Sign out/in and revisit **Profile → Devices**.
- **No push delivery?** Verify push is enabled in **Settings → Notification Providers**.
