# Teams

Teams group users by responsibility and ownership.

## Why Teams Matter

Teams help you:

- Assign service ownership
- Route incidents effectively
- Manage permissions at scale

## Create a Team

1. Go to **Teams**
2. Click **+ New Team**
3. Add a name and description

## Team Roles

| Role   | Permissions                         |
| ------ | ----------------------------------- |
| Owner  | Full control, can delete the team   |
| Admin  | Manage members and settings         |
| Member | Standard access                     |

## Add Members

1. Open the team
2. Go to **Members**
3. Click **+ Add Member**
4. Assign a role

## Assign Services

1. Open a service
2. Go to **Settings**
3. Select the owning team

## Best Practices

- Keep teams aligned to real ownership boundaries.
- Assign every production service to a team.
- Use teams in escalation policies.
