## Dashboards

- Incident volume trends
- MTTA/MTTR charts
- Team performance
- Service health
- Escalation step distribution

## Filters

Filter by:

- Time range
- Services
- Teams
- Severity
- Priority

## Reports

### Executive Summary

Highlights:

- Total incidents
- SLA compliance
- Top affected services
- Period-over-period trends

### On-Call Performance

- Response times by responder
- Incidents per shift
- Escalation frequency

## Exporting Data

1. Go to **Analytics**
2. Apply filters
3. Click **Export**
4. Download CSV or JSON

## Best Practices

- Review weekly to spot patterns.
- Track trends, not just totals.
- Share reports with stakeholders.
