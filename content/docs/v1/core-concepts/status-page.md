# Status Page

Share service health and incident updates with customers and stakeholders.

## What the Status Page Shows

- Live service status
- Incident history
- Maintenance windows
- Uptime metrics

Default URL: `https://your-ops.com/status`

## Incident Updates

1. Open the incident
2. Click **Add Status Update**
3. Publish customer-friendly notes

Suggested phases:

- Investigating
- Identified
- Monitoring
- Resolved

- Post updates quickly and consistently.

- Publish postmortems for major incidents.
